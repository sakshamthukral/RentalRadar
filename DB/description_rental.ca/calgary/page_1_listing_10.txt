Broadstreet's Livingston Heights offers 1, 2, and 3 bedroom, pet friendly apartments for rent in Calgary's new North, the beautiful community of Livingston. Connected to Calgary and the surrounding area, Livingston Heights is situated near Stoney Trail, Deerfoot Trail, Highway 2, and the Calgary International Airport.
Close by you’ll find shops, restaurants, grocery stores, and fantastic community features like parks, playgrounds, and sports areas. Livingston Heights offers a vibrant rental community with features like a spacious, open-concept layout, modern finishings, five full-size appliances, and a private outdoor space in every suite. On-site, you’ll also find our signature dog run and dog wash station.
Tenants will also have access to the Livingston Hub, a 35,000 sq. ft. fully managed indoor/outdoor facility complete with a banquet facility, commercial kitchen, café, gathering spaces, gymnasium, tennis courts, hockey rink, amphitheatre, playground, splash park, basketball court, and more! It doesn’t get much better than this with a team of 24/7 support staff designed to ensure your comfort and convenience. On-site management and maintenance are just another reason to call Broadstreet’s Livingston Heights your home.
