The Pinnacle at Bridgwater is a pet-friendly community that offers spacious one bedroom + den and two bedroom + den apartments for rent in the Bridgwater Trails neighbourhood of south Winnipeg.
Suites are fully equipped with central heat, air conditioning, full-size stainless-steel appliances (including dishwasher and microwave), in-suite laundry and upgraded modern finishes such as quartz countertops and luxury vinyl plank flooring.
Rent includes water, in-suite laundry and exclusive access to a resident clubhouse.
Inquire about subsidized suites at The Pinnacle at Bridgwater.
* Same-day application approvals are available.
* Rates are based on a one-year lease and may vary by floor/layout.
* Reduced security deposits for seniors.
* Pet restrictions apply.
