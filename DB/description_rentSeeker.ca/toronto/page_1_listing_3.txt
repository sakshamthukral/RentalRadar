2283 Eglinton Avenue East offers apartments for rent in Scarborough at Kennedy & Eglinton. Our large one, two, three and four bedroom suites offer comfort and convenience to our residents, with a great location minutes away from transit and shopping. With a Walk Score of 67 and excellent Transit Score of 96 Kennedy subway station and the Kennedy Go Station is conveniently just a short walk away. Residents also have the luxury of being located minutes away from great shopping at Eglinton Town Center, and dozens of other retailers along Eglinton Ave. East. Or if you prefer the great outdoors, then visit Eglinton Ravine Park located just outside the residence across the street!
